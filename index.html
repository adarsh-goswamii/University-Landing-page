WELCOME !

Bansal Institute of Science and Technology, Bhopal established in 2000 with a vision to provide national & international academic inputs to nurture & prepare technocrats capable of growing and contributing in fast changing global environment. The Institute brings to the students an integrative learning model based on industry needs of skilled professionals. The model provides these complementary skills sets to the students at various leveraging domains & functional & cultural expertise. The cultural skills is to be built through academic collaboration internationally, nationally & locally.

The Institute was started by Shriniwas Education Society, Bhopal. The founder Chairman, Late Shri K.C. Bansal had a vision to make Professional Education affordable to all the sections of the society and established the Institute in 2000.

VISION

To see an India free from poverty and illiteracy where the poor are empowered, an India were the bridge between the poor and the rich is within acceptable limits. An India in which today’s youth succeed tomorrow, create a niche space for themselves, and are able to contribute to the growth of the country.

MISSION

  • To meet the growing demand for technocrats with required professional knowledge and skills.
  • To create an educational environment in which students are prepared to meet the challenges of growing needs of the society by inculcating in them updated technical knowledge, analytical and practical skills.
  • To develop a constructive and positive out look in life among the students.
